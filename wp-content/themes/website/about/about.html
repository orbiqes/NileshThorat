Why Bundle?.

We handle all areas of garment finishing in-house, meaning we quality control at every stage to ensure your workwear is to the highest of standards. Our Bundles are made up of the most popular items sold at Jaybee Supplies, grouped together so you can save money whilst being able to personalise and order the right colour and size for you and your colleagues.

Ordering workwear doesn’t need to be hard work and with our Bundles you are able to choose the right combination for you and add your own logo. We embroider your logo so it is a true representation of your branding and stands the test of time.

All of our Bundles are £119.99+VAT, with 5 out 5 on our Facebook reviews! We have a monthly special Bundle that is £99+VAT – keep an eye out for these deals.
